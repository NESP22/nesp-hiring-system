# NESP Platform Setup Checklist

Use this before any job ad goes live.

## NESP Careers Website

- Account needed: NESP OpenCATS admin.
- Permissions needed: job posting/admin access.
- Billing needed: no.
- Automation: internal posting only.
- Verification: no external identity check.
- Craig provides: final public job details.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves public role.
- Removal/expiration: set job inactive or not public.

## Indeed

- Account needed: Indeed employer account.
- Permissions needed: employer posting access.
- Billing needed: only for sponsored posts.
- Automation: treat as manual unless a reviewed integration is approved.
- Verification: Indeed may require employer verification.
- Craig provides: account access, posting category, paid/free decision.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves before publish or sponsorship.
- Removal/expiration: close, pause, or expire post in Indeed.

## Facebook

- Account needed: NESP Facebook page admin access.
- Permissions needed: page posting or ads access.
- Billing needed: only for boosts/ads.
- Automation: manual post or Meta tools only after approval.
- Verification: Meta may require account or business checks.
- Craig provides: page access, image approval, free/paid decision.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves post and any spend.
- Removal/expiration: delete post, stop boost, or pause campaign.

## Instagram

- Account needed: NESP Instagram or connected Meta account.
- Permissions needed: posting or ads access.
- Billing needed: only for promotion.
- Automation: manual post/story/reel.
- Verification: Meta may require checks.
- Craig provides: account access, approved image, caption approval.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves post and any spend.
- Removal/expiration: delete post/story or stop promotion.

## LinkedIn

- Account needed: LinkedIn company page or recruiter access.
- Permissions needed: company page/job posting access.
- Billing needed: only for paid jobs or promoted posts.
- Automation: manual unless a reviewed integration is approved.
- Verification: LinkedIn may require business or admin verification.
- Craig provides: page access, role category, free/paid decision.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves before publish or payment.
- Removal/expiration: close, pause, or remove post.

## Craigslist

- Account needed: Craigslist account.
- Permissions needed: posting access for the target region/category.
- Billing needed: likely yes for job posts.
- Automation: manual only.
- Verification: Craigslist may require account, phone, or payment checks.
- Craig provides: account access, region/category, payment approval.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves each paid post before payment.
- Removal/expiration: remove or let expire in Craigslist.

## Google Business Profile

- Account needed: Google Business Profile manager access.
- Permissions needed: profile post access.
- Billing needed: no for standard posts.
- Automation: manual.
- Verification: Google may require business/profile verification.
- Craig provides: profile access, post approval, image approval.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves post.
- Removal/expiration: remove post or let it age out.

## College Career Boards

- Account needed: varies by school.
- Permissions needed: employer posting access.
- Billing needed: usually no, sometimes yes.
- Automation: varies; assume manual.
- Verification: schools may require employer review.
- Craig provides: target schools, account/contact, approved copy.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves board terms and posting.
- Removal/expiration: close post or let board expiration pass.

## Photography Schools And Programs

- Account needed: school contact or employer account.
- Permissions needed: varies.
- Billing needed: usually no.
- Automation: manual outreach or form.
- Verification: varies by program.
- Craig provides: approved contact list and copy.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves outreach.
- Removal/expiration: request removal or follow school process.

## Local Community Groups

- Account needed: group membership or page access.
- Permissions needed: posting permission.
- Billing needed: no unless boosted.
- Automation: manual only.
- Verification: group rules vary.
- Craig provides: approved groups and post copy.
- Preview before publishing: yes.
- Approval step: Craig approves each post.
- Removal/expiration: delete post or follow group rules.
