# Recommended 20-Minute Craig Walkthrough Agenda

## 0-3 Minutes: Dashboard Orientation

- Log in.
- Open **NESP Hiring**.
- Explain **Needs Craig**, **Phone Screens**, **Job Ads**, and **Settings**.

## 3-7 Minutes: Applicant Review

- Open a fake or sanitized applicant.
- Show application details and prescreen answers.
- Explain that every decision is human-reviewed.

## 7-11 Minutes: Scheduling Link

- Click **Invite to Schedule Phone Screen**.
- Review safety settings.
- Generate a scheduling link.
- Copy the invitation text.
- Explain manual sending only.

## 11-14 Minutes: Candidate Scheduling And Outcomes

- Show what candidates see.
- Explain Eastern Time, no recording, automated call notice, and consent before transcription.
- Show scheduled, completed, no-answer, cancelled, reschedule, and human follow-up states.

## 14-17 Minutes: Availability And Emergency Stop

- Open availability settings.
- Show blocks, blackout dates, buffers, notice periods, daily limits, and link expiration.
- Show where to turn calling off with `NESP_VAPI_ENABLED=0`.

## 17-20 Minutes: Job Ads

- Open **Job Ads**.
- Show tracked links, ad drafts, source reporting, and manual spend fields.
- Repeat stop gates: no publishing, paid campaigns, billing, terms, accounts, or messages without explicit approval.
