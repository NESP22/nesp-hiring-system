# Vapi Consent And Transcription

## Rule

Audio recording must remain disabled. Text transcription is retained only after clear affirmative consent.

## Required Opening

```text
Hi, this is the automated New England Sports Photo hiring assistant regarding your application.

This call will not be audio recorded, but it will be transcribed into text so the NESP hiring team can review your job-related responses. A person makes every hiring decision.

Do you consent to continue with this transcribed phone screening?
```

## Refusal Or Unclear Consent

If consent is unclear or refused:

- do not continue screening
- do not pressure the caller
- offer human follow-up
- end politely
- store only consent refusal and call metadata
- do not retain a substantive transcript
- do not change candidate status

## Allowed Review Content

After affirmative consent, Craig may review the permitted transcript, factual structured answers, unanswered questions, and suggested human follow-up questions.

Do not analyze accent, tone, emotion, speech speed, personality, or protected characteristics.
