# NESP Application Prescreening Questions

The NESP careers portal is the single application destination for external
recruiting. Each approved public role has job-specific prescreening questions on
the NESP application form.

Applicants see this statement above the questions:

`These questions help the NESP hiring team understand your availability and job-related qualifications. Every application is reviewed by a person.`

## Storage And Review

- Answers are posted with the normal OpenCATS application form.
- Answers are logged to OpenCATS candidate questionnaire history as
  `NESP Prescreen - [role title]`.
- Craig can review answers from the candidate profile questionnaire results.
- Answers are not used for automatic rejection, ranking, hiring, assignment,
  applicant-status changes, or email.
- The implementation does not ask about age, date of birth, race, religion,
  disability, medical conditions, marital or family status, or other protected
  characteristics.

## Roles Covered

- `41001` Part-Time Customer Service Representative
- `41002` Weekend Staff Portrait & Team Photographer - Youth Sports
- `41003` Freelance/Contract Youth Sports Photographer
- `41005` Weekend Table Greeter / Field Assistant

Job `41004` remains inactive/nonpublic and has no NESP prescreen questions.

## External Posting Rule

Do not duplicate these prescreening questions inside Indeed, Craigslist,
Handshake, college boards, MassHire, or other external platforms when the
platform can send candidates to the matching NESP application link.

If a platform requires its own application workflow or prevents the direct NESP
application URL, stop before publication and get Craig's approval.
