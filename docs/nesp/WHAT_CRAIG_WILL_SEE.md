# What Craig Will See

## When Phase 2 Flags Are Off

Craig sees standard OpenCATS behavior. Phase 2 dashboard surfaces stay disabled unless their feature flags are enabled.

Admin Settings remain available so Craig can review disabled flags and safety status.

## When Dashboard Foundation Is Enabled In Preview

Craig sees a `NESP Hiring` workspace with:

- `Needs Craig`
- `Waiting`
- `Interviews`
- `Completed`
- `Staffing Forecast`
- `Settings`

The first screen is task-oriented rather than menu-oriented.

## Candidate Cards

Cards show:

- Candidate name.
- Role.
- Stage.
- Waiting on whom.
- One factual summary.
- Last activity.
- One primary next action.
- Secondary links for detail/audit context.

## Interviewer Follow-Through

Settings show:

- Inactive interviewer profiles.
- Suggested routing rules.
- Manual candidate grants.
- Availability blocks.
- Scorecards.
- Unlock controls for submitted scorecards.
- Audit events.

## Staffing Forecast

The staffing forecast page is planning-only. It shows fixture/sanitized forecast structure until Craig approves real historical schedule import.

It does not publish jobs, edit job records, create applicants, send messages, or enable integrations.

## What Craig Will Not See Yet

- Real Vapi phone calls.
- Real Zoom meeting creation.
- Real AI candidate review.
- Real Google Drive staffing import.
- Automatic email or SMS.
- Automatic ranking, rejection, hiring, offer, or status changes.
- Real interviewer access without separate approval.
