# Craig Daily Hiring Checklist

Use this once or twice per hiring day.

## Morning

- Log into the NESP hiring dashboard.
- Open **Needs Craig**.
- Review new applicants.
- Decide which applicants should receive **Invite to Schedule Phone Screen**.
- Copy and send approved invitations manually.
- Open **Phone Screens**.
- Check **Phone Screens Today**.
- Check **No Answer / Reschedule Needed**.
- Check **Human Follow-Up Requested** items in phone-screen details.

## Midday

- Confirm no urgent no-answer or cancellation items are waiting.
- Review newly completed phone screens.
- Read transcripts only after consent is shown.
- Add Craig review notes.
- Decide the next human step.

## End of Day

- Review **Upcoming Phone Screens**.
- Confirm tomorrow's availability is correct.
- Add blackout dates if needed.
- Check that audio recording still shows off in Vapi configuration status.
- Keep `NESP_VAPI_ENABLED=0` if scheduled calling is not approved for production use.

## Never Do From This Checklist

- Do not publish job ads.
- Do not activate paid campaigns.
- Do not send automated email or SMS.
- Do not change Vapi recording settings.
- Do not change candidate stages automatically.
- Do not modify Render or database settings without a controlled task.
