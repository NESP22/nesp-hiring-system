## Publication Approval Checklist

Before any external post goes live, Craig should confirm:

- Platform and account are correct.
- Role, pay, territory, schedule, classification, equipment, and application
  link are correct.
- Staff photographer and freelance photographer are clearly separate.
- Contractor posting does not describe employee benefits as contractor benefits.
- No applicant email-resume request is added.
- No unsupported claims such as `#1` or `premier` are used.
- No local-review or draft wording is present.
- No paid sponsorship, boost, billing, CAPTCHA, identity verification, or final
  publish step is pending without Craig present.
- No automatic applicant ranking, rejection, hiring, status changes, or email is
  enabled.
