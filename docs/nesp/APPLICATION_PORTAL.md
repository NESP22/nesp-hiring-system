# NESP Application Portal

The NESP careers portal presents four protected job listings while Basic Auth remains enabled.

## Current Public Roles

- Part-Time Customer Service Representative
- Weekend Staff Portrait & Team Photographer - Youth Sports
- Freelance/Contract Youth Sports Photographer
- Weekend Table Greeter / Field Assistant

## Portal Requirements

- The careers board must show exactly four public jobs.
- Application links remain controlled by OpenCATS and must not be replaced with live external job-board links.
- Applicant communications remain draft-only and require human approval.
- Hiring decisions remain human-reviewed.
- No automatic rejection, selection, hiring, status changes, assignments, or outbound email are enabled.
- Do not add real applicant data during testing or review.

## Role Routing

### Part-Time Customer Service Representative

Route candidates who are interested in in-person Methuen office customer service, weekday daytime schedules, customer emails, support tickets, calls, order questions, reprints, replacements, and coordinator support.

### Weekend Staff Portrait & Team Photographer - Youth Sports

Route candidates interested in W-2 weekend photographer assignments where NESP provides equipment and paid training.

### Freelance/Contract Youth Sports Photographer

Route experienced photographers who provide their own approved professional equipment and want seasonal contractor assignments.

### Weekend Table Greeter / Field Assistant

Route candidates interested in active weekend field support, family check-in, team organization, order-form review, posing-guide support, and photographer assistance.

## Review Policy

AI may summarize job-related facts, identify missing information, and draft interview questions or messages for human review. NESP management makes every interview, assignment, and hiring decision.
