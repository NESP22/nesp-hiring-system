# Craig First Week Checklist

## Before Inviting Candidates

- Confirm the production deployment is approved.
- Confirm migrations were applied in a controlled task.
- Confirm `NESP_VAPI_ENABLED` is still off until calling is approved.
- Confirm audio recording is off.
- Confirm transcription is after consent only.
- Confirm availability windows are correct.
- Add blackout dates for days Craig does not want calls.
- Review the copy-only invitation text.

## First Small Batch

- Choose a small group of applicants.
- Generate scheduling links.
- Copy and send invitations manually.
- Watch **Waiting to Schedule**.
- Confirm candidates can pick Eastern Time appointments.
- Confirm no email or SMS is sent by the system.

## First Scheduled Calls

- Turn scheduled calling on only after explicit approval.
- Watch **Phone Screens Today**.
- Confirm one call attempt per appointment.
- Check for no-answer outcomes.
- Do not automatically reject anyone.
- Add Craig review notes after completed calls.

## First Job-Ad Setup

- Use the **Job Ads** control center to copy draft ads.
- Use tracked links for each platform.
- Keep paid platforms in draft until Craig approves budget and billing.
- Record manual spend only after Craig spends money outside the system.

## End Of Week Review

- Review applications by source.
- Review completed phone screens.
- Review no-answer and reschedule needs.
- Confirm all hiring decisions were made by a person.
- Confirm no Customer Service Vapi resources were changed.
