# Staff Photographer — School & Sports Photography

**New England Sports Photo — W-2 Position**  
**Seasonal with recurring assignments and growth potential**  
**Proposed compensation: approximately $22–$27/hour based on assignment level and experience; confirm before posting**

New England Sports Photo is hiring dependable staff photographers for school and youth-sports picture days throughout New England.

This is a W-2 position for photographers who want recurring assignments, established systems, training, and production support from a local company rather than having to find every client and manage the entire business themselves.

NESP is family-run, locally trusted, and has photographed athletes and students since 1975. Our photographers work with schools, leagues, coaches, families, and internal production teams to deliver a consistent, professional photo-day experience.

## What You’ll Do

- Photograph individual students, athletes, teams, coaches, and staff
- Set up and operate assigned cameras, lighting, backgrounds, and related equipment
- Follow NESP standards for exposure, focus, posing, crop, naming, and file handling
- Direct children and groups clearly while maintaining a positive environment
- Work efficiently enough to keep scheduled photo lines moving
- Coordinate with assistants, table staff, school personnel, coaches, and team leads
- Confirm that required poses and group combinations are completed
- Identify and report roster, scheduling, equipment, or customer issues
- Protect image files and complete required transfer and end-of-assignment procedures
- Participate in training, quality review, and performance feedback

## What We’re Looking For

- Strong attendance, punctuality, and professional communication
- Photography experience with solid control of exposure, focus, and lighting
- Comfort directing children, teenagers, adults, and groups
- Ability to learn and consistently follow NESP’s volume-photography workflow
- Reliable transportation and willingness to travel to assigned locations
- Ability to carry, set up, and break down photography equipment
- Availability for selected school-day, evening, and weekend assignments
- Ability to remain calm and solve problems during fast-moving events
- Sports, school, portrait, event, studio, or volume-photography experience is preferred

## Employment Structure

- W-2 employment
- Assignments vary by season, client schedule, experience, and location
- School photography may include weekday mornings and daytime hours
- Sports photography frequently includes evenings and weekends
- Additional assignments and lead responsibilities may be available to photographers who demonstrate consistent quality and reliability

## Why Work With NESP

- Established clients and recurring assignments
- Training and documented photo-day procedures
- Support from assistants, table staff, schedulers, customer service, and an in-house production operation
- Opportunities to grow into lead photographer and larger-account responsibilities
- Work for a local family-run company serving New England communities
