# NESP Seasonal Hiring Ads

These are review-ready drafts for New England Sports Photo. They are not published automatically.

## Brand position

NESP should present itself as the local, family-run alternative to large national and regional school-photo companies. The public job ads should focus on NESP's own strengths rather than naming or criticizing competitors:

- Family-run and locally trusted since 1975
- Sports and school photography throughout New England
- Professional photo-day systems and training
- In-house production and quality control
- Seasonal work with opportunities to return
- Real responsibility and advancement for dependable team members

## Roles included

1. Freelance Sports & School Photographer — independent-contractor assignments
2. On-Site Photo Day Table Team Member — seasonal W-2 position
3. Photography Assistant / Poser — seasonal W-2 position
4. Staff Photographer — W-2 school and sports photography position

## Compensation assumptions to confirm before posting

These drafts use the current internal planning rates available to the hiring project:

- Lead / experienced photographer: approximately $27 per hour
- Second photographer / photography assistant: approximately $22 per hour
- Table / posing staff: approximately $20 per hour

Craig must approve the final classification, pay rate, mileage/travel policy, minimum call time, and overtime rules before any advertisement is published.

## Safety and hiring rules

- Do not automatically reject candidates using AI.
- Do not score protected characteristics or proxies such as age, name, photo, address, graduation year, disability, family status, race, ethnicity, religion, sex, or gender.
- AI may summarize job-related experience and flag missing information for human review.
- Independent-contractor classification must be reviewed for the actual work arrangement before the freelance role is posted.
