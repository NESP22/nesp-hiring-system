# Methuen Operations Support

**New England Sports Photo - Methuen, Massachusetts operations role**  
**Pay: hourly rate pending final Craig/payroll approval**  
**Location: Methuen, Massachusetts**  
**Primary season: September through November, with possible work before and after peak season**

New England Sports Photo is hiring operations support help for our Methuen office during the busy fall picture-day season.

This role supports the behind-the-scenes work that keeps picture days moving: preparing materials, organizing job details, checking paperwork, supporting production flow, and helping the office team stay caught up during peak season. It is a good fit for someone reliable, detail-oriented, comfortable with computers, and able to work steadily through repetitive but important tasks.

## Schedule

- Primary fall season is September through November.
- Hours may vary by workload, picture-day schedule, and production needs.
- Some weekday daytime or afternoon availability is expected.
- Occasional evening or weekend support may be requested during peak season.
- Final schedule, pay, employment classification, and payroll terms are pending approval before posting.

## What You Will Do

- Prepare, sort, label, and organize picture-day paperwork and supplies.
- Help confirm job details, rosters, forms, and internal notes.
- Support office and production teams with data entry and checklist follow-up.
- Organize materials before and after picture days.
- Help track missing information and route issues to the right NESP team member.
- Assist with basic quality-control checks and production support tasks.
- Keep work areas, paperwork, and job materials organized.
- Follow privacy rules for customer, league, school, and applicant information.

## What We Are Looking For

- Strong reliability, attendance, and follow-through.
- Careful attention to names, numbers, dates, forms, and written details.
- Comfort using a computer, email, spreadsheets, and web-based systems.
- Ability to stay organized while handling repetitive tasks.
- Clear communication when information is missing or unclear.
- Ability to work from the Methuen office as scheduled.
- Respect for confidential customer, youth-sports, school, and applicant information.
- Office, production, data-entry, customer-service, school, sports, or operations experience is helpful.

## Why Work With NESP

- Seasonal operations role with a local family-run company.
- Work that directly supports schools, leagues, families, photographers, and production.
- Training on NESP systems and picture-day workflows.
- Opportunity to return for future seasons or grow into more office, scheduling, production, or customer-support responsibility based on reliability and performance.
