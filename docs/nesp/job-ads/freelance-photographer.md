# Freelance/Contract Youth Sports Photographer

**New England Sports Photo - Seasonal contract assignments**  
**Pay: $22-$27 per hour, based on experience, pending final payroll/legal classification confirmation**  
**Locations: Massachusetts, Rhode Island, New Hampshire, and Connecticut**  
**Primary season: September through November**

New England Sports Photo is building a network of experienced freelance photographers for youth sports picture-day assignments across New England.

This role is for photographers who already operate with professional camera equipment and want recurring seasonal assignments with an established local company. NESP manages the client relationship, schedule, products, production, and customer service. You bring reliable professional equipment, follow the assignment workflow, and deliver consistent images.

## Classification Note

This listing is draft-only and must not be published until the final independent-contractor classification review is complete for the actual work arrangement and state where the work is performed.

## Schedule

- Most fall assignments run September through November.
- Weekday assignments are usually after approximately 4:00 PM.
- Weekend assignments are generally early morning through noon.
- Assignments are offered based on location, experience, availability, and client schedule.
- Paid time begins when you leave home and continues through completion of the assignment.
- Return travel is paid when the drive home exceeds one hour.

## What You Will Do

- Photograph individual athletes, teams, coaches, and league staff.
- Arrive on time with professional working equipment and backup equipment.
- Follow assignment-specific posing, lighting, naming, and file-handling standards.
- Direct children and groups clearly, respectfully, and efficiently.
- Keep photo lines moving while maintaining image quality.
- Work professionally with NESP leads, assistants, table staff, coaches, and league contacts.
- Communicate quickly if traffic, weather, schedule, or equipment issues arise.
- Protect image files and transfer completed work according to NESP procedures.

## Required Equipment

Contract photographers must provide their own professional equipment, including:

- Professional DSLR or mirrorless camera body.
- Appropriate portrait and sports lenses.
- External flash or lighting equipment suitable for the assignment.
- Memory cards, batteries, chargers, and basic field supplies.
- Backup camera equipment in case of failure.

Assignment-specific equipment expectations will be confirmed before booking.

## What We Are Looking For

- Strong portfolio or sample images showing relevant work.
- Experience with portrait, sports, school, event, studio, or volume photography.
- Professional reliability and communication.
- Comfort directing children, teenagers, adults, and teams.
- Understanding of exposure, focus, flash, and image consistency.
- Reliable transportation to assigned photo locations.
- Availability on selected weekday evenings and weekend mornings during the fall season.
- Ability to complete required background-check steps for field work.

## Why Work With NESP

- Recurring seasonal assignment opportunities.
- Organized clients, schedules, and picture-day procedures.
- Production, printing, products, and customer service handled by NESP.
- Local family-run company serving New England communities since 1975.
- Opportunity for more assignments based on quality, reliability, and availability.
