# Freelance Sports & School Photographer

**New England Sports Photo — Seasonal Assignments Across New England**  
**Independent-contractor assignments | Weekday and weekend opportunities**  
**Proposed compensation: approximately $27/hour for qualified lead assignments; confirm before posting**

New England Sports Photo is looking for dependable freelance photographers to join our network for youth sports and school picture days throughout Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Rhode Island, and Connecticut.

NESP is a family-run photography company serving New England since 1975. We provide established photo-day systems, organized schedules, production support, and an experienced local team. This is a strong fit for photographers who enjoy working with children, teams, schools, and community organizations and who want recurring seasonal assignments without having to find and manage every client themselves.

## What You’ll Do

- Photograph individual athletes, teams, students, and staff using NESP’s established workflow
- Arrive on time, prepared, and professionally dressed for each assignment
- Follow posing, lighting, camera-setting, naming, and file-handling standards
- Keep photo lines moving while maintaining a positive experience for children and families
- Work closely with NESP leads, assistants, table staff, coaches, and school personnel
- Protect equipment and image files and transfer completed work according to NESP procedures
- Communicate immediately if weather, traffic, equipment, or scheduling issues arise

## What We’re Looking For

- Strong reliability and professional communication
- Experience with portrait, sports, event, volume, or school photography
- Confidence directing children and groups efficiently
- Working knowledge of exposure, focus, lighting, and basic troubleshooting
- Reliable transportation and willingness to travel to assigned locations
- Ability to carry and set up photography equipment
- Availability for selected weekdays, evenings, and weekends during busy seasons
- A professional DSLR or mirrorless camera system is preferred; assignment-specific equipment requirements will be confirmed before booking

## Why Work With NESP

- Recurring seasonal assignments
- Organized schedules and established photo-day procedures
- Support from a local family-run company with decades of experience
- Opportunities to move into lead assignments based on performance and reliability
- No need to sell packages, collect clients, print products, or run your own customer-service operation

## To Apply

Submit your résumé or work history, location, travel radius, equipment list, portfolio link or sample images, and current weekday/weekend availability.

### Application Questions

1. What city and state are you based in?
2. How far are you willing to travel for an assignment?
3. Which weekdays, evenings, and weekends are you generally available?
4. Describe your portrait, sports, school, event, or volume-photography experience.
5. What camera bodies, lenses, flashes, lighting equipment, and backup equipment do you own?
6. Are you comfortable photographing and directing children and groups?
7. Do you have reliable transportation?
8. Provide a portfolio link or upload 5–10 relevant examples.
9. Have you worked under another company’s posing, naming, and file-delivery standards?
10. Tell us about a time you solved an equipment or schedule problem during a job.

## Human Review Criteria

- Reliability and communication
- Relevant image quality and consistency
- Ability to follow a repeatable workflow
- Comfort directing children and groups
- Equipment readiness and backup plan
- Travel radius and seasonal availability
