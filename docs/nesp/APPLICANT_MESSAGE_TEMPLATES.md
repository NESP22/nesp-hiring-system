## Messaging Rules

- Human approval required before every send during the first deployment phase.
- Do not include AI scores or internal review notes in applicant communications.
- Do not promise an interview, assignment, hours, benefits, compensation, or employment unless approved.
- Do not send rejection messages based only on an AI routing bucket.
- Do not request Social Security numbers, bank details, identification documents, background-check information, or medical information through ordinary email or text.
- Do not expose applicant email addresses or phone numbers to other applicants.
- Log the approved template version and the human approver.
